Data Protection

Detailed information on data protection, technical security, and creator rights.

01. Responsibility

The responsible party for data processing on this website is the operator named in the Imprint. We take the protection of your personal data very seriously and treat it confidentially in accordance with the GDPR.

02. Technical Data & Hashing

Server Log Files

Our hosting provider automatically collects information that your browser transmits (Browser type, OS, Referrer URL, Time). This is necessary for technical stability.

Showcase Interaction (Privacy-First)

To ensure a fair system and prevent spam, we track code interactions anonymized: Your IP address is instantly converted into an anonymous cryptographic hash (SHA-256). We do not store your original IP address. This hash is automatically deleted after 24 hours.

03. Encryption & Fonts

SSL/TLS Encryption

This site uses SSL/TLS encryption to protect the transmission of confidential content. You can recognize this by the "https://" in the address bar.

Google Web Fonts

To ensure a consistent visual experience, this site loads web fonts provided by Google. Your browser connects to Google's servers to load these fonts into your cache.

05. Your Rights

You have the right to be informed about your personal data at any time. Since we only store anonymized hashes for 24 hours (no cookies, no accounts), our data storage is minimized to the technical absolute necessity.
